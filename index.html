Welcome to Acta Diurna, the free educational resource for staying up-to-date on world events.


1- Learn

Want to know the way the economy works? Understand the Affordable Care Act? Know the conflicts in which the United States is still involved? Browse the toolbar above, search for a recent news article, or use the categories below to start learning about the world around you (features to be added).

2- Play

Once you have started a subject, reinforce what you have learned through games and quizzes. Compete directly with your friends and win points for maintaining daily streaks. New material added every day! (features to be added).

About Acta Diurna: Acta Diurna allows you to learn the stories behind the articles you read in the newspaper or hear on the news. Whether it's relevant the biography of a famous person or key terms in the economy, Acta Diurna makes sure you learn the world around you. Most importantly, it makes staying informed fun. From short explanatory videos to interactive games and resources, Acta Diurna will make you an educated citizen and further your understanding of the current events. Check out the Acta Diurna mobile app (coming soon) and Browser Explainer (coming soon) to keep Acta Diurna with you wherever you go.
